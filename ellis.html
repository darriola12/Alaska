Elli's Family

Wants to hear a fairy tale?

Fortunately, this isn't a tale of magic spells and enchanted forests, but it's a delightful story about the day I crossed paths with some incredible Alaskan folks.
Back in 2020, I received a call to embark on a full-time missionary journey in Canada, Winnipeg. Imagine my excitement when I cracked open that missionary assignment letter! With just six months to prepare for the adventure ahead, I found myself facing a mountain of gear to gather. Being a missionary in Canada meant I needed loads of thermal clothing, and as you can guess, it wasn't exactly budget-friendly. With no job and my parents unable to foot the bill for all my gear, you can imagine my relief when an old friend reached out with unexpected news. It turns out there was a missionary group itching to lend a hand with my gear. And here's where the plot thickens â€“ by some stroke of luck, Jen and Brant were vacationing in Costa Rica at the time, and they were eager to chip in. Talk about serendipity in action! I'll never forget the day I welcomed them into my home. There was something special about Jen and Brant from the get-go. Throughout my mission, we kept in touch, and before I knew it, they were extending an invitation for me to stay with them in Alaska for a few months. Can you believe it? If this isn't straight out of a fairy tale, I don't know what is!

About Alena

Alena holds a special place in my heart from my time in Alaska. I'll never forget the first day we met, when she and her friends decided to host an impromptu concert upstairs. She's the youngest daughter of the family, and her room was conveniently located right across from mine. One night, in the middle of the night, I decided to play a harmless prank on her. I placed a tiny pillow in front of her door, knocked, and then dashed away. Little did I know, it sparked a pillow war between us that ended with a mountain of pillows in front of her door. Alena has a unique way of showering people with love and kindness, and she's equally unique in how she expresses her affection for those she cares about. Many of my nights were spent upstairs, offering Alena love advice as she navigated matters of the heart. And let me tell you, she's a wizard in the kitchen when it comes to baking cookies. During those seemingly endless, boring nights, Alena was always there as my little sister, bringing joy and laughter to even the dullest moments.

About Ethan

Ethan is my other best friend from Alaska, and he's a sixteen-year-old guy with a heart of gold. The first time I met him, he was quiet and introverted, but he showed kindness towards me from the start. My goal was to build a strong friendship with him, and it didn't take long for us to hit it off. A couple of weeks later, we ventured outside for the first time to play football. Ethan absolutely loves football and dreams of becoming a star quarterback and playing in college. I have no doubt he can achieve it, although he probably already knows it won't be easy. He enjoys spending time outdoors, especially with his buddies Walter, Wyatt, and his other friends. Every time I snuck into the kitchen for a late-night snack, Ethan was always there, glued to the TV. I guess I never managed to grab my snack because I was too embarrassed to disturb him. I came to Alaska during the season of love, as both Alena and Ethan had fallen for someone. Many nights were spent upstairs, chatting about the rollercoaster of emotions that comes with love. Ethan has a heart of gold, and I feel grateful to have met such an awesome guy."

About Brant & Jen

Those two incredible souls you see in the picture are Brant and Jen, and let me tell you, they have hearts of gold. When I first arrived in Alaska, winter was slowly bidding farewell, but Brant made sure to brighten my mornings before heading to the shop. No matter where he was, he managed to bring a smile to my face. Brant is passionate about his work and excels in business. I'm certain he instills the values of hard work and diligence in his children. He's also a bit of a car enthusiast, but above all, his love and dedication to his family always shine through. And then there's Jen, Brant's wonderful wife. Let me tell you, her heart is as big as the Alaskan wilderness. Throughout my stay in Alaska, she was my pillar of support, always ready to lend a helping hand and shower me with love. She was like a second mother to me during those days. Jen has a passion for Zumba and is quite the pro at it. Her love for her family knows no bounds, and she's always there to take care of them. I'll never forget the time I fell ill after work and stumbled back to their house. Jen nursed me back to health all through the night, and the next morning, I found a little cup with pills and my name on it. It may have been a simple gesture, but it brought tears to my eyes. I felt an overwhelming amount of love from her. Saying goodbye to Brant and Jen was one of the hardest things I've ever had to do, but I know wherever they go, they continue to spread love and kindness, touching the lives of everyone they meet.

About Camry

Camry is the quintessential middle child of the family, and let me tell you, she's a force to be reckoned with. I worked alongside her every day, and I can say with confidence that she's an incredible woman. Like Brant, she's also crazy about cars and once owned a beautiful Mustang. I'll never forget the time we went to see a theater play based on Aladdin, and Camry portrayed Jasmine. At first, I was skeptical because I had seen Aladdin plays before, and they were rather dull. But this one was a whole different ballgame. Camry was ridiculously awesome; her performance and the special effects left me in awe. Aside from her passion for cars and acting, Camry also has a love for volleyball. I never told her, but I secretly wanted to join her in a game because I love volleyball too. Perhaps due to both of us being young individuals living in our own worlds, we didn't form a particularly strong bond. However, she was always there to lend a helping hand with my work and offer support. This woman holds a special place in my heart.

About Garret & Carlieann

Garret was another one of my closest friends, serving not just as a mentor and boss, but as a true older brother figure. He arrived in Alaska just a week after I did, and to my surprise, he spoke Spanish! I grew to appreciate Garret immensely, especially for his infinite patience with me at work. I vividly recall those initial days on the job when he generously treated me to lunch, until he realized that continuing to do so might lead us straight to bankruptcy! Despite his quiet demeanor, Garret was full of fun ideas and had an unwavering love for his brothers. He was also on the verge of marrying Carlieann, a sweet and kind-hearted girl with a passion for running. I don't know her secret, but she could run for miles on end, and when I say a lot, I mean a lot! Garret, to me, was more than just a friend; he was an older brother from whom I learned countless valuable lessons. He might not have been aware, but I always looked up to him, seeing a radiant Christ-like light shining from his face.

About Branden

Branden, the eldest son of the family, left quite the impression on me from the moment we met. Though our time together was limited, I quickly noticed his kind heart and impressive cooking skills. He's passionate about two things: cars and girls. I often joked that I should have asked him for flirting advice because he seemed to have it all figured out! One memorable experience was when we both attended the Single Adults class in the stake. Though we left feeling a tad disappointed, it was a blast meeting new people with him by my side. Branden also became my go-to basketball buddy. We'd hit the court together from time to time, and let me tell you, it was always a riot. He's the type of guy who loves to embark on crazy and cool adventures, things that I probably wouldn't dare to do myself. I have no doubt that Branden has a bright future ahead of him.
