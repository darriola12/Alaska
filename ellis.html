Elli's Family

Wants to hear a fairy tale?

Fortunately, this isn't a tale of magic spells and enchanted forests, but it's a delightful story about the day I crossed paths with some incredible Alaskan folks.
Back in 2020, I received a call to embark on a full-time missionary journey in Canada, Winnipeg. Imagine my excitement when I cracked open that missionary assignment letter! With just six months to prepare for the adventure ahead, I found myself facing a mountain of gear to gather. Being a missionary in Canada meant I needed loads of thermal clothing, and as you can guess, it wasn't exactly budget-friendly.
With no job and my parents unable to foot the bill for all my gear, you can imagine my relief when an old friend reached out with unexpected news. It turns out there was a missionary group itching to lend a hand with my gear. And here's where the plot thickens â€“ by some stroke of luck, Jen and Brant were vacationing in Costa Rica at the time, and they were eager to chip in. Talk about serendipity in action!
I'll never forget the day I welcomed them into my home. There was something special about Jen and Brant from the get-go. Throughout my mission, we kept in touch, and before I knew it, they were extending an invitation for me to stay with them in Alaska for a few months. Can you believe it? If this isn't straight out of a fairy tale, I don't know what is!
