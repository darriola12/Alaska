You know what...

Before traveling to Alaska many people told me that:

  • 1. The Yeti lives there: Believe or not, everyone thinks that big foot lives in the Alaska Mountains, it is ashamed I never saw him
  • 2. It's always dark or always light: Actually this is kinda true, my first nights in alaska there was not darkness
  • 3. It's always cold: The belief that Alaska is always cold is common among people, especially due to its reputation for extremely cold winters.
    Can you believe me if I tell you that I usually wear short pants to go out, sometimes that was a bad idea?
  • 4. It's a remote and wild place: Many people have the impression that Alaska is a remote and wild place, far from civilization and full of pristine nature.
